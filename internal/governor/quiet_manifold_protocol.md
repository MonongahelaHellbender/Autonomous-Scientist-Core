# PROTOCOL: The Quiet Manifold (Local Social Safety)

## Objective
To ensure that Carbon Capture Gigafactories in the East Texas corridor contribute to regional peace rather than disrupting it.

## Constraints
1. **Acoustic Invariant:** Total factory noise floor must not exceed 42 dB at the property line (equivalent to a quiet library).
2. **Visual Invariant:** Light pollution must be directed downward with a 100% 'Dark Sky' compliance to protect the local environment.
3. **Vibrational Shielding:** Using the same Phononic Bandgap tech from Phase 6 to cancel out industrial pump hum before it reaches the soil.

## Community Dividend
For every 1 Mt of carbon captured, the system must provide a 'Peace Surplus'—funding for local quiet-infrastructure (parks, walking trails, and prairie buffers).
