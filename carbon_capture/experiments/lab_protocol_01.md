# EXPERIMENTAL PROTOCOL 01: Bio-Mimetic Calcium-Cage Synthesis

## Objective
Synthesize CaC2-Variant featuring an 82% efficiency topological gate via Silicon boundary doping.

## Phase 1: Precursor Preparation
1. Isolate high-purity Calcium Carbide (CaC2) baseline matrix.
2. Prepare organosilicon precursor for vapor deposition.

## Phase 2: High-Temperature Calcination & Doping
1. Elevate furnace to 582°C (baseline stability threshold).
2. Initiate Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) of Silicon.
3. **CRITICAL:** Calibrate deposition rate to achieve exact gradient of 0.0343 mol/cm³ at the crystalline boundary.

## Phase 3: Adversarial Validation
1. Subject synthesized samples to rapid thermal cycling (Peak: 750°C, Delta: 150°C).
2. Monitor via X-ray Crystallography for core manifold degradation.
3. Target Failure Rate: < 0.01%.
