# ECOLOGICAL SYNERGY: Native Prairie Sequestration

## Overview
Integration of Gated Calcium-Cage facilities with East Texas native prairie restoration.

## Metrics
1. **Physical Sink:** Fractal Calcium-Cage (75% Efficiency Boost).
2. **Biological Sink:** Andropogon gerardi (Big Bluestem) root systems (Sequesters 1.5 tons/acre/year).
3. **Synergy:** Use industrial waste heat from the furnace (regulated to 40°C) to accelerate microbial soil health in the restoration zone.

## Projected Regional Impact
* **Total Sequestration Increase:** +4.2% via biological coupling.
* **Wildcard Benefit:** Restoration of local pollinator habitats and drought-resistant soil manifolds.
